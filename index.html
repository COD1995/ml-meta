  • As a M.S. candidate in Mechanical Engineering, Kristopher Kodweis engineers intelligent computer vision systems designed to run efficiently on mobile platforms, enabling a new generation of smart and responsive applications. His engineering philosophy is uniquely shaped by a background in visual communication design and woodworking, instilling a deep focus on human-centered principles.
